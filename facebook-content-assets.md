- Primary Gold: #C9A96E (champagne)

## Post Types & Frequency

**Recommended Weekly Breakdown:**
- Product Spotlights: 3-4 posts (Carousel format)
- Educational Posts: 2 posts (Tips, guides, trends)
- Blog Snippets: 1-2 posts (Drive traffic to blog)
- Engagement Posts: 1-2 posts (Polls, questions, community)
- Sales/Promotions: 1 post (When applicable)

**Total: 8-11 posts per week (1-2 daily)**

## Engagement Best Practices

1. **Respond to comments within 2 hours** during business hours
2. **Encourage user-generated content** (ask followers to tag themselves)
3. **Use polls and questions** to boost engagement
4. **Share customer testimonials** and features
5. **Post consistently** (1-2 times daily)
6. **Use call-to-action buttons** on every post
7. **Monitor analytics** and adjust strategy based on performance
8. **Engage with similar brands** (like, comment on related jewelry pages)

---

## Monthly Performance Targets

- Reach: 5,000+ people
- Engagement Rate: 3-5%
- Click-Through Rate: 2-3%
- Follower Growth: 200-300 new followers
